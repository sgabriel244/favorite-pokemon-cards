Fossil Set is the third set released for the Pokémon Trading Card Game in late 1999 (in English). It shows some of the fossil Pokémon Kabuto, Kabutops, Omastar, Omanyte and Aerodactyl, the legendary birds Articuno, Zapdos and Moltres and Pokémon interacting with their natural environment.

The packs covers are Zapdos, Aerodactyl and Lapras.

This is the last expansion to feature the Galaxy Star Holofoil before being replaced by the Cosmos holographic pattern.

Pack release date:
Japanese: June 21, 1997
English: October 10, 1999
