The Base Set is the first series of the Pokémon Trading Card Game (TCG), released in 1999. It introduced players to the world of Pokémon cards, featuring iconic Pokémon such as Charizard, Blastoise, and Venusaur. The set includes a variety of card types, including Pokémon, Trainer, and Energy cards, and is known for its nostalgic value and classic artwork. The Base Set laid the foundation for the Pokémon TCG, making it a beloved collection among fans and collectors alike.

Its cards are characterized by their simple design, with a focus on the Pokémon's illustration, basic stats, and moves. The set also introduced the concept of rarity, with cards classified as Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Holo Rare. The Base Set remains a significant part of Pokémon TCG history and continues to be cherished by enthusiasts around the world.

The main Pokémon shown on the pack images above are Charizard, Venusaur, and Blastoise, which are the final evolutions of the three starter Pokémon from the original Pokémon Red and Blue video games: Charmander, Bulbasaur, and Squirtle, respectively.

Pack release date:
Japanese: October 20, 1996
English: January 9, 1999
